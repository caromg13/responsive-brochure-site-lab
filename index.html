ABOUT MANSE

The word Manse means a large and impressive residence. No matter what size your residence is, Manse can assist you in creating a look and feel just right for each room in your home.

Recognised nationally and internationally, Manse Furniture has spent more than 20 years curating a diverse portfolio spanning high end residential, retail, hospitality and corporate projects. More recently the studio, guided by founder and managing director Jo Manse, has expanded into five-star hotels, custom multi-residential interiors, external design and furnishings.

Manse is very passionate about home furnishings and we choose our brands carefully to ensure we are providing our customers with high quality, innovative and modern designs for their homes.

“Our focus is to inspire our clients with a finished project that goes beyond their expectations. Our philosophy is to produce unique and individual design results that not only reflect our clients or their brand but enhances their lives.”
